Using Impact Maps for planning and controlling process improvements

v0.1

Let's start with an analysis what information is needed in regards to possible process improvements:

  • What are we going to do?
  • What problem is solved by that?
  • Who do we need in order to help us?
  • How do we know if we were successful?
  • What do we need in order to do the process improvement?
