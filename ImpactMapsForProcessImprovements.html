Using Impact Maps for planning and controlling process improvements

v0.1

Motivation

The typical outcome of retroperspectives should be process improvements. Books like Agile Retroperspectives and Gamestorming offer a wide range of ideas for conducting these meetings. To enable the team to envision improvements more methological and strategical often used methods are lacking.

Hypothesis

Impact Maps are a method to strategically develop products or on a wider scale strategically deliver solutions to problems. As process improvements are also solutions to problems Impact Maps might also help in retroperspectives.

Analysis of Impact Maps

Impact Maps are composed of four different layers:

  • Why are we doing this?
  • Who is going to help/hinder us?
  • How can they help/hinder us?
  • What do we need to do in order to enable/disable the "How"?
A more detailed explanation can be found at http://impactmapping.org.
